• about us

    With a passion for helping those less fortunate than us, we started our journey in 2017 with the help of just a few friends and supporters. We started off with a basic aim, to make sure that the surplus food in hotels, restaurants and function halls don’t go in the trash, rather be fed to the needy. Over the span of 3 years, this sporadic activity became a full-time nation-wide organization beyond just feeding leftovers to the needy.
