OUR WORK

By the grace of God and the generosity of our donors!

ANAJ BANK - Feed the needy

started in May 2017, our aim with this initiative is to make good use of the surplus food in hotels, function halls etc by distributing it in orphanages, govt hositals, night shelters, bus stands and similar places

we also do food distribution programmes as per our donor requests

DRINKING WATER projects

DAILY FREE DRINKING WATER PROJECT @ NILOUFER HOSPITAL - started in Dec 2017, we have manageed to arrange a water tanker with 1000 litre water distribution daily

DRINKING WATER CAMP @ GOVT HOSPITAL OF ZAHEERABAD - besides free drinking water inside the premises, ORS is distributed on a daily basis to the patients.

BOREWELL PROJECTS - a total of 7 borewells have been dug till date and open for public use in the states of Telangana ( Khammam, Kamareddy ) and Andhra Pradesh ( Prakasam)
